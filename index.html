The changes

As we have seen for ourselves that covid 19 has affected the world in both good and bad ways, once the virus was announcrd to the world in late december of 2019, we never knew how it could change our lives. Some people say it was a way of nature to balance the world and decrease pollution, Even though covid 19 was devastating, the only countries that had no cases at all were North Korea and Turkmenistan, surprisingly the Wuhan province was on of the areas in China with the least infected people. Now the virus has changed the way people view the world due to people in lockdown forced to get updates from social media. It has also helped us research about pandemics and how to stop infection. In an unbased opinion, the virus had discouraged us but helped us to learn to never quit and look for a ray of hope in dark times.
